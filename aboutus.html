Who We Are?

QAL Greens (Pty) Ltd is a company specializing in Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency projects. Our organization helps with the implementation of a green and energy efficient environment in the residential, commercial and industrial business.
We do energy saving consultations and renewable energy design, supply and installations.

Our Mission

To find and implement solutions to the development of energy savings and renewable energy generation, delivering clean, cost effective renewable energy power. We strive to innovate and champion energy to the residential, commercial and industrial business in the whole of South Africa.

Business Objectives

Our main objectives are to offer services that help reduce or eliminate monthly payments on electricity and produce energy with the process void of any smoke, gas, or other chemical by-product as nations attempt to meet climate change obligations in carbon emissions.

Vision

Our vision is to see a strategic innovative change to energy usage and renewable energy to help reduce costs and carbon emissions for the better living of our future generations.
